# Company Knowledge Base

## Return Policy
Customers may return any product within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Items must be in original condition with the original receipt.
Electronics require original packaging to be eligible for return.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the item is received.

## Shipping Policy
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days across Kazakhstan.
Express shipping (2-3 days) is available for an additional 2,500 KZT fee.
Same-day delivery is available in Almaty and Astana for orders placed before 12:00 PM.
Free shipping applies to all orders over 30,000 KZT.

## Warranty Policy
All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty.
Accessories have a 6-month warranty from the date of purchase.
Warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship only.
Physical damage and water damage are not covered under warranty.
To file a warranty claim contact support with your order number and a photo of the defect.

## Payment Methods

Installment plans (рассрочка) are available via Kaspi Bank for orders over 50,000 KZT.

## Laptop Pro X1 Setup Guide
Charge the laptop fully before first use (approximately 2 hours).
Press the power button to start and follow the on-screen setup wizard.
Download the latest drivers from support.example.kz/drivers using your serial number.
The serial number is located on the sticker on the bottom of the device.
Factory reset can be performed by holding F9 during startup.

## USB-C Hub Compatibility
The hub works with any USB-C port that supports DisplayPort Alternate Mode.
Supports up to 4K at 60Hz via the HDMI port.
Requires USB 3.1 Gen 1 or higher for full data transfer speed.
Not compatible with USB-C charging-only ports (common on older Android phones).
Maximum power delivery passthrough is 85W.
